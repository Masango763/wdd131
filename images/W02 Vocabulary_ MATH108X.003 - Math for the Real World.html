W02 Vocabulary

Overview

  • Task: Make sure you understand the definitions of these words, study them if necessary.
  • Purpose: Help increase the reading comprehension for the material being covered this week.

Vocabulary List

Arithmetic [noun]
The branch of mathematics dealing with the properties and manipulation of numbers.
Calculations [noun]
The mathematical determination of the size or number of something.
Cash Flow [noun]
The difference between your income and your expenses.
Constancy [noun]
The quality of being enduring and unchanging.
Conversion [noun]
In math, conversion is the process of changing the value of one form to another
Decimal [noun]
Decimal numbers lie between integers and represent numerical value for quantities that are whole plus some part of a whole.
Division [noun]
The action of separating something into parts or the process of being separated.
Expense [noun]
The cost required for something; the money spent on something.
Frugal [adjective]
Careful in spending or using supplies; simple and without unnecessary things.
Inaccurate [adjective]
Not exact or correct, having inconsistencies.
Itemized [verb]
To present as a list of individual items.
Multiplicative [adjective]
Tending or having the ability to multiply; increasing more and more over time.
Prorate [verb]
Change the time frame in expenses or income.
Quotient [noun]
A result obtained by dividing one quantity by another.
Reevaluate [verb]
To evaluate again or differently.
Savings Withdrawal [noun]
The act of taking money out of a savings account. A savings account is a bank account that earns interest.
Substituting [verb]
To use or add one thing in place of another.
Unit [noun]
A single thing, person, or group forming part of a whole.

Study Tips

  • Make flashcards yourself with physical materials.
    • There are online apps to allow you to create online flashcards. However, research has proven that handwritten flashcards improve retention over using an app.
  • Read the assignments and try to understand what the words mean. The reading assignments for this week include the words you are required to study.
