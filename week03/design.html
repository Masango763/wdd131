Core Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy & Whitespace

Establishing a clean hierarchy of information helps users rapidly digest the relative importance of different structural blocks on a page. By combining targeted typographical sizing with strategic chunks of empty layout whitespace, you guide the user's natural viewing focus seamlessly down a page without overwhelming them.

Key Takeaway

Design for the user and your business objectives. Effective web design relies heavily on psychological readability principles applied directly to layout alignment, consistent type treatments, and high contrast thresholds.

Evaluation Checklist

  • Typography Limits (≤ 2 Fonts)
  • Harmonious Palette (≤ 5 Hues)
  • Accessible Contrast (≥ 4.5:1 Ratio)
  • Semantic Element Selection
  • Consistent Multi-column Alignment
